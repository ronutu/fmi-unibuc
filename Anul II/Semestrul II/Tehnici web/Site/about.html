ABOUT
AIRMAX IS A LIFESTYLE BRAND GRASS ROOTED IN WEST COAST STREET CULTURE. FOUNDED BY ARTIST YG, WE'RE BRINGING A RAW, EDGY AND UNAPOLOGETIC MESSAGE.

THE PURPOSE OF THE RELAUNCH WAS TO BRING THE BRAND BACK TO IT'S ROOTS, RE-FOCUS ON WHAT REALLY MATTERS AND BUILD THE ALL STAR TEAM NEEDED TO DELIVER BETTER QUALITY PRODUCT. NOW WE'VE DONE THAT, SO HERE WE ARE.

THIS COLLECTION WAS INSPIRED BY THE DAILY MORALS AND LIFESTYLE OF LA STREET CULTURE.

CENTERED AROUND THE AIRMAX'S DEFINITION OF GANGSTA, EVERYTHING HAS MEANING AND PURPOSE. FROM THE CONSIDERED COLOR COORDINATION ON THE STRIPE PIECES TO THE F STITCH ON THE PANTS, EVERYTHING IS DONE FOR A REASON.

THE NEW COLLECTION IS FULLY KUT & SEWN, MADE OR CONSTRUCTED IN LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA USING CUSTOM FITS THAT REFLECT THE STREET CULTURE AND THE LIFE WE LIVE!
